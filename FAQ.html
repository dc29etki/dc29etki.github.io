When a resident purchases a policy from Effective Coverage there is no action you or your residents need to take. All policy information is automatically tracked and stored in Louie, the Effective Coverage platform. Residents that purchase Effective Coverage policies do not need to submit it to be approved, or have the additional interest added.
When any of your residents purchase a policy from Effective coverage an email will be sent notifying you that a resident has purchased insurance through us. The email will include basic policy details, but there is no action you need to take.
